Welcome to Wise Guys Barbers!

Wise Guys is your local Gents' barbers. In business since 2001, and located at the junction of Southdown Road and Piggottshill Lane, in Southdown, Harpenden, we offer a friendly, professional haircutting service. Please do feel free to drop by - no booking required.

Please note that, regretfully, we cannot accommodate babies or toddlers (even well-behaved ones!)
