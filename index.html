Welcome to Wise Guys Barbers!

Wise Guys is your local Gents' barbers. In business since 2001, and located at the junction of Southdown Road and Piggottshill Lane, in Southdown, Harpenden, we offer a friendly, professional haircutting service.

I am happy that we have been able to relax some of the measures put in place during the COVID-19 outbreak; however we are maintaining frequent cleaning of the premises and sanitisation of all equipment between each booking.

I am continuing to take online bookings only. This ensures there is adequate time allocated to each customer, and no-one is waiting around.

  • Online bookings (appointments only)

I also continue to accept card payments only (no cash) for everyone's convenience.

Thank you for your valued custom - it is very much appreciated!
